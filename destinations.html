Destinations · The Route Network

Four terminals. Infinite gates.

Departures subject to the shape of reality, the mood of the deities, and your own readiness, which we will assess honestly and charge for regardless.

Terminal I

Past Lifetimes

Regression Service

Round-trip service into your own back-catalog. We do not promise you were anyone important; statistically you were not, and there is a strange relief in confirming it. Popular routes: The Life You Almost Remember · The One That Explains the Fear · A Life in the Age of Taurus (seasonal).

Flight data: duration 20–90 min · re-entry assisted · souvenir memory included.

Terminal II

Parallel Dimensions

The Sideways Earths

Lateral travel to the Earths adjacent to this one. Economy sideways-Earths differ from yours by a single decision; premium routes diverge at the level of physics. Please note that some travelers prefer the destination to the origin, and we are not liable for reluctance to return.

Flight data: β-class and above · disorientation common · anchor-line provided.

Terminal III

The Angelic Switchboard

Angelic Contact

Direct-dial the eight intelligences that line the temple. Each call connects you to one downregulated faculty of your own soul.

  • · Beauty — the alarming kind; brings your grief up with it.
  • · Mystery — will not answer directly; that is the point.
  • · Eros — beyond the health protocol; beyond the mind.
  • · Madness — enlivening, contained only by the body of the Calf; travel supervised.
  • · Compassion — to suffer with, not to dissociate from.
  • · Courage — “with heart”; comes with vulnerability as standard equipment.
  • · Truth — vertical, narrative, humble; requires surrender.
  • · The Eighth — the mirror. You will be assigned this gate and will not be told; you supply its name.
Terminal IV

Deity Concierge

The Theogony

Introductions to the marching band of related gods, arranged by our clergy. Roster includes the Calf / the First God (the alpha and omega; audience by invitation only, Throne Class), El of Canaan, Shiva the meditating monk, Dionysus the hedonist, and Hermes, patron of our ground crew, who stole the first cattle and invented the sacrifice. Deities are contacted at their discretion and may decline.

Flight data: outcomes not guaranteed · offerings recommended · attention is the only currency they accept.
