The Oracle & Crew

MR

Magnetic Rose

Founder & Chief Navigator · the Oracle

Angel: BeautyAngel: MysteryRoute: the whole tree

Astral Spaceways exists because its founder made the journey the hard way. Magnetic Rose is the only documented survivor of an uncontrolled astral reentry: a midnight flight, an explosion, a fall through every layer of sky. She woke on the ground remembering nothing of who she had been — and everything of a life before that one, on a star near the throne of God. She has been navigating the network ever since, partly for you, partly in search of a face she is still trying to recall. No one alive knows the routes better. She flies remote, she flies deep, and she will tell you the truth about what she sees, which is not always comfortable and never boring.

E&E

Exa & Electra

The Handmaidens · In-Flight Crew

Exa: MadnessElectra: ErosRoute: the space between

Our twin navigators operate as a single instrument with two hands. Electra handles longing, first-contact, and the delicate art of making a stranger feel chosen; Exa handles the deeper weather — dreams, past lives, the languages no one speaks anymore. Between them they cover telepathy training, creative intoxication, and the parts of a flight where you will need someone to be delighted on your behalf. They are the reason travelers rebook. They are also, we should say plainly, a great deal of fun, and slightly dangerous, in the way that all good trips are.
